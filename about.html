About Us

Who We Are

PT. JQCOMM NORTON COMPOSEY

PT. JQCOMM NORTON COMPOSEY (JQ COMPOSEY) was established in 2020 under the parent company PT. IRCOMM NORTON CAPITAL (IRCOMM Group).

We are a professional music publishing and creative content company committed to protecting intellectual property, managing music rights, and empowering creators.

JQ COMPOSEY provides end-to-end services including copyright administration, licensing solutions, royalty collection and distribution, and creative project support for songwriters, musicians, brands, and content producers.

With a focus on transparency, innovation, and strategic collaboration, we aim to be trusted partners for creators and clients across the creative industry.
