About Us

Your trusted partner in music publishing, copyright management, and creative content development

Est. 2020

PT. Melodex Sonavia International

PT. Melodex Sonavia International (MELODEX) was established in 2020 under the parent company PT. IRCOMM NORTON CAPITAL (IRCOMM Group).

We are a professional music publishing and creative content company committed to protecting intellectual property, managing music rights, and empowering creators.

MELODEX provides end-to-end services including copyright administration, licensing solutions, royalty collection and distribution, and creative project support for songwriters, musicians, brands, and content producers.
